Welcome to Playgrounds! This Privacy Policy governs your use of the Playgrounds mobile application ("App"), a map-based social network developed by Fluxe Labs, based in Toronto, Canada. We are committed to transparency and security, but your responsibility in providing accurate information and securing your account is critical. Read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate Playgrounds:

  • Post Location Data: The geographic location you attach to posts, collected only with your explicit consent before using the App. This is used solely to display your posts on the map and may be visible to other users as part of the App’s core functionality. We do not collect or track your live location.

We do not collect payment information or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed here unless explicitly stated in future updates.

We use your data exclusively for:

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose not explicitly stated here.
